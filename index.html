The Problem

Your mailbox is overflowing—and so are landfills

Every year, billions of pieces of unsolicited mail flood American households, wasting resources and your time.

80B+
Pieces of junk mail per year

The US alone generates over 80 billion pieces of junk mail annually—most of it goes straight to the trash.

40+ lbs
Per household each year

The average American household receives more than 40 pounds of junk mail every year, consuming time and space.

Producing and recycling junk mail consumes an estimated 28 billion gallons of water annually.
